Meet Our Global Lead Pastors

Pastor Sunday Oziegbe Favour, as the Global Lead Pastor, is a transformative leader, teacher, and global conference speaker. His commitment to empowering leaders and making a positive impact on societies is evident through initiatives like the Nehemiahs Family and events such as the Exponential Conference and Gravity.

Pastor Blessing Favour, the Global Co-Lead Pastor, brings over a decade of finance experience and a heart for women's empowerment. She pioneers Jewels, the Women's Ministry, and hosts the annual Made for More Conference, equipping women with spiritual and practical tools for success.

Together, Pastor Sunday and Blessing Favour lead Faith Miracle Assembly Church's global expansion, impacting lives across continents and cultures.

The Faith Miracle Assembly Church is supported by a global team of Pastors and Ministers who are committed to advancing God's kingdom across the nations. Our leadership team brings together diverse gifts, experiences, and callings, all united under the vision of seeing lives transformed by the Gospel of Jesus Christ.
