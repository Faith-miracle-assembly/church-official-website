Join the Nehemiah’s Family!

Just as Nehemiah rose with courage to rebuild the walls of Jerusalem, we too are called to build lives of purpose, unity, and spiritual strength. The Nehemiah’s Family is more than a community—it’s a divine assignment.

We believe in the power of godly connections, the beauty of shared vision, and the strength of a loving spiritual family. Here, you'll find passionate believers walking together in faith, just as Nehemiah rallied his people to restore what was broken.

Join us to experience spiritual growth, divine restoration, and meaningful relationships. Together, we rise to build, to heal, and to fulfill destiny.

Meet Our Global Lead Pastors

At the heart of Faith Miracle Assembly Church are our visionary leaders — Pastor Sunday Oziegbe Favour and Pastor Blessing Favour — who guide our global family with wisdom, grace, and unwavering faith.

Pastor Sunday Oziegbe Favour

A dynamic author, inspirational speaker, and expert in leadership and relationships. His teachings empower individuals to lead boldly and live purposefully.

Pastor Blessing Favour

A celebrated publisher and finance strategist, known for her passion for women's empowerment. She leads the transformative Made for More conference, inspiring women to embrace their full potential.

Together, they are supported by a dedicated team of pastors and ministers across the globe, united in the mission to serve both God and humanity with passion and grace.
