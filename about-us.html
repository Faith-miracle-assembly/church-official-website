Our Story

Faith Miracle Assembly (GRACE HOUSE) was founded on June 25, 2017, under the divine leadership of Global Lead Pastor, Pastor Sunday Oziegbe Favour. Born from a vision received through the guidance of the Holy Spirit, this assembly stands as a sacred call from God—inviting men to rise and be restored to their original identity in Him. At GRACE HOUSE, we believe in the transformative power of faith, prayer, and supplication. This is a place where grace empowers believers to carry spiritual burdens and walk boldly in purpose. Led by the Holy Ghost, we are a forward-thinking teaching ministry committed to restoring men back to God. Through biblical wisdom, practical insight, and principle-based teachings, we equip individuals to live lives of significance—first on earth, and ultimately in heaven.
